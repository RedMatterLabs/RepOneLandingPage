For the purposes of this document, the App and the virtual coaching interface are collectively called “Platform.”
By visiting our Website and/or using our Services on the Platform, you are agreeing to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
The Information We Collect
1. Personal Information
To help organize your organization, we collect additional information that you voluntarily provide to us. These include, without limitation, email addresses, organization names, coach names, athlete names, and athlete images.
All information collected pursuant to this section is collectively referred to as “Personal Information.” We do not collect or access any Personal Information from you when you use the Services unless you provide us with the Personal Information voluntarily or authorize us to access it.
2. Lifting Metrics
The Platform provides you with high quality lifting data. In order to provide visualization of such data, we collect multiple metrics from the RepOne Device through WiFi. The metrics may include, without limitation, velocity, positional data, and range of motion. The RepOne Platform also calculates and collects relevant metrics, which may include, without limitation, rest times, set numbers, and rep numbers. All metrics mentioned in this section are collectively called the “Lifting Metrics.”
3. Workout Information
To help organize your Lifting Metrics, we collect additional information that you voluntarily provide to us. These include, without limitation, exercise names, RPE, weights, and reps. In addition, we also collect information about the Mobile Device that logged the Lifting Metrics, such as the platform (e.g., iOS or Android) and the version of the App. Such information is collectively called the “Workout Information.”
4. Activity Information
We may collect activity and usage information occurring via the Platform, collectively called the “Activity Information”. Such information may include, without limitation, modifying Settings, connecting to an RepOne Device, logging in an athlete, editing a set, ending a set, ending a workout, and syncing with the cloud.
5. Other Information
We may collect additional information (collectively, the “Other Information”). Such Other Information may include tokens to assist in 3rd party services such as push notifications.
